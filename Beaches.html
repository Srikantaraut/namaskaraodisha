Puri Sea Beach

Puri Beach is a beach in the city of Puri,this is the land of Jagannath. One of the char dham. Famous peligrim destination. It is on the shore of the Bay of Bengal. Amidst the tranquillity of the beach and divinity of this sacred venue, a beach festival is organised every November that attracts tourists from across the world and makes Puri Beach is a perfect place to visit.The beach hosts sand art displays, including work by international award-winning local sand artist Sudarshan Pattnaik. Puri to konark ~30km merine drive one such road any driver would never miss. A holy dip in sea then Jagannath darshan consider very auspicious among Hindu.
