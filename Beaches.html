Puri Sea Beach

Puri Beach is a beach in the city of Puri,this is the land of Jagannath. One of the char dham. Famous peligrim destination. It is on the shore of the Bay of Bengal. Amidst the tranquillity of the beach and divinity of this sacred venue, a beach festival is organised every November that attracts tourists from across the world and makes Puri Beach is a perfect place to visit.The beach hosts sand art displays, including work by international award-winning local sand artist Sudarshan Pattnaik. Puri to konark ~30km merine drive one such road any driver would never miss. A holy dip in sea then Jagannath darshan consider very auspicious among Hindu.

Konark Sea Beach

Konark Sea Beach. Just 3 Km away from the Sun Konark Temple, Konark Beach in Odisha (Orissa) is a nirvana for a beach lover since a beach visitor can simultaneously bathe in the warmth of the sun and witness the beauty of sunrise or sunset. Another beautiful sight is that of the spotting fishermen, grossly involved in their daily chores with the fish. Famously know as "Chandravaga", where soon set and sun rise can be seen together.

Gopalpur Sea Beach

Once a commercial port, used for trading during East India Company Days. A perfect amalgam of history to a modern day point of attraction. You can enjoy hot fresh cooked sea food served by local's with a soothing beauty of bay of bengal.

Chandipur Sea Beach

The beach is unique in that the water recedes up to 5 kilometers during the ebb tide. Due to its unique circumstances, the beach supports bio-diversity.Watching the sea vanish right in front of your eyes and then observing its return is a very rare site. And to add more to this hide and seek of water, the Casuarina trees and creeper sand dunes seem to be swaying on a melodious musical note
